About us

Local flavours, warm service

Bean Cafe opened to bring small-batch coffee and home-style bakery to Sector 22, Chandigarh. We source ingredients locally when possible, support neighbourhood suppliers, and run weekly community meetups. Open Mon–Sun for breakfast and evening coffee breaks.
